About Us

The Meeks are a group of 4 who formed a few years ago after all 4 members attended MAINZ (Music and Audio Institute of New Zealand). Since coming back to Wellington from Auckland they have decided that they are going to concentrate fully on pursuing a musical career.

Initially starting out by doing covers of popular songs, they started writing and performing their own songs much to the delight of their fans.

Their music style is a true fusion of many different types of music and they have not settled on a single style, nor do they feel any particular urgency to do so in the near future.

  • Mischa - Vocals / Guitar
  • Jack - Piano / Keyboard / Ukele / Melodica
  • Gavin - Guitar / Drums
  • Ed - Guitar
